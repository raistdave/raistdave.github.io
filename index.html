About

8i

8i creates photorealistic human holograms for virtual, augmented and mixed reality. As a computer vision researcher in this innovative field, my mandate is to improve our acquisition and refinement of colour and depth. This brings together approaches from diverse fields such as image processing, vision, rendering and machine learning.

Visit the company website

Rendering Engine

Realtime raytracing is challenging. To facilitate the use of GPU resources, API models propose heterogeneous programming schemes allowing for GPGPU via the launch of parallel compute kernels. I chose CUDA to build a rendering engine, intending to keep up with state-of-the-art techniques including acceleration structures, sampling, global illumination and antialiasing.

Light transport

My Master’s thesis focuses on the concepts and equations of light transport, based on a set of radiometric formulations describing visible light. It proposes a numerical technique to estimate the frequency spectrum of scattering light. As part of my studies, I have also worked on simulations using MPM, structured light scanning and various rendering techniques.
